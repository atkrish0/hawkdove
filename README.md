# HawkDove: AI-Powered Central Bank Insights

## Overview

HawkDove is a platform that leverages Large Language Models (LLMs) to analyze and extract key macroeconomic insights from central bank communications. By processing policy statements, meeting minutes, and economic reports, HawkDove provides a structured understanding of monetary policy trends and economic indicators.

This system is designed to help traders, economists, and policymakers interpret monetary policy shifts and their potential impacts on financial markets. By leveraging AI, HawkDove enhances transparency in central bank communication and assists users in making data-driven decisions.

## Features

- **Sentiment Analysis:** Detects hawkish versus dovish tones in central bank language, helping users gauge policy shifts.
- **Macroeconomic Insights:** Extracts discussions on inflation, interest rates, GDP, employment, and oil prices from official documents.
- **Interactive Dashboards:** Uses Plotly Dash to visualize trends in monetary policy and economic indicators.
- **Cited Responses:** Provides direct references to original publications, including source, author, and page information, ensuring transparency.
- **Live Querying:** Uses a Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) system to answer user prompts based on textual data from central banks.

## Data Sources

HawkDove processes and analyzes publications from major central banks, including:

- **Federal Reserve** – Monetary policy statements, FOMC minutes, speeches, and economic outlook reports.
- **Bank of England** – Monetary Policy Reports, interest rate decisions, and official meeting summaries.
- **European Central Bank** – Economic Bulletins, Governing Council press releases, and speeches.
- **Bank of Japan** – Policy Board statements, inflation reports, and financial system reviews.
- **Reserve Bank of India** – Monetary Policy Statements, RBI Governor speeches, Bi-monthly Monetary Policy Committee (MPC) meeting minutes, Financial Stability Reports, and RBI Bulletins.

By continuously ingesting and analyzing these sources, HawkDove enables users to stay informed about global macroeconomic conditions and anticipate policy shifts.

## Notes & Known Issues

### ChromaDB Integration

When using ChromaDB within Dash applications, database reset or deletion operations may cause unexpected issues. To mitigate this, ensure that all VectorDB instances are properly terminated before attempting deletion. Alternatively, direct usage of the `chromadb` library can provide enhanced control over database operations.

### Data Retrieval Limitations

Frequent requests to certain central bank servers, such as the Bank of England, may result in temporary access limitations. To address this, users may need to adjust request frequencies or implement IP rotation strategies if accessing data programmatically.
